Our Story

Heritage & Tradition

Nestled in the heart of Mylapore, Haritam stands as a testament to South India's rich culinary heritage. Our restaurant was born from a passion to preserve and celebrate the authentic flavors of traditional vegetarian cuisine while embracing modern standards of excellence.

Every dish at Haritam tells a story - from recipes passed down through generations to innovative creations that honor our roots while delighting contemporary palates.

Our Philosophy

We believe that food is not just sustenance but a celebration of life, culture, and community. Our commitment to using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients ensures that every meal is not only delicious but also nutritious and sustainable.

The rustic architecture of our space, with its traditional elements and warm ambiance, creates an environment where families gather, friends reconnect, and memories are made.
